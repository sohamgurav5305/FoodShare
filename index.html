About FOODSHARE

Built to turn rescued food into dependable community care.

Founded in 2020, FOODSHARE started as a local response to food insecurity and grew into a practical support system connecting donors, volunteers, and neighborhoods.

We work with businesses, farms, organizers, and residents to make sure edible food is not wasted and that families receive help with dignity.

Local pickup coordination for food donors
Food support requests for families and organizations
Community-first approach focused on speed and respect

About Us

Food relief shaped by community trust.

We are building a food support network that respects people’s dignity, reduces waste, and keeps care rooted in the neighborhoods we serve.

Mission

Reduce food insecurity by rescuing edible surplus and redistributing it to people and organizations who need it most.

Vision

A city where no one goes hungry and food support is responsive, accessible, and community-led.

Values

Compassion, dignity, sustainability, urgency, and partnerships that make local care stronger every week.

Journey

How FOODSHARE grew.

What began as a small grassroots effort during a crisis has become a broader platform for donations, requests, and coordinated support.

Started local

Meals were first distributed directly to nearby families experiencing sudden need during the pandemic.

Built partnerships

Local restaurants, businesses, and volunteers joined in to create a wider and more dependable support network.

Moved online

The website now helps donors and applicants connect with the organization through clearer, faster digital workflows.
